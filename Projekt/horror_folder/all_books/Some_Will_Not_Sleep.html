Some Will Not Sleep: Selected Horrors

Adam Nevill

In ghastly harmony with the nightmarish visions of the award-winning writer's novels, these stories blend a lifelong appreciation of horror culture with the grotesque fascinations and childlike terrors that are the author's own. Adam Nevill's best early horror stories are collected here for the first time.

A confused and vengeful presence occupies the home of a first-time buyer . . .
